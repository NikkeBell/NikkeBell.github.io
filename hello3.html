Why does this website exist?

How does this website work??

This website lists all of the nearest restaurants of the Gofore office in Lappeenranta. In the first box you can see a link, it will lead you to a list of all the restaurants and information about them.
The link is the last word in the first box. At the bottom of the page you can find links to Gofore's home page and to a finnish translation of this page.
